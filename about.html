About Us

Highlights

  • 17 successful years in the field of education
  • We have accomplished an astonishing growth from mere 16 students to thousands of students over the years.
  • We have a staff of more than 50 people including teaching & non-teaching members.
  • We conduct about 500 lectures per week at all our branches.
  • There are in all 5 branches of YASHI COACHING CLASSES covering Hindi & English medium of school section & science, commerce & professional courses streams of college section.

MISSION

"To Nurture talents and bring out the best of everyone."

Thus, the Basic objective of establishing this Institute was neither commercial nor merely to earn a livelihood. The Basic Objective was to satisfy the inner urge of dedicating oneself to the sincere academic pursuit and to guide the eager young students with a missionary zeal, to enhance their worthiness and imbibe the nobler values of life in their receptive minds not by empty words but by one’s own example.
